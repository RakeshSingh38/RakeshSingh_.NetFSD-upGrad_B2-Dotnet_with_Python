Our Infrastructure

The campus houses multiple academic blocks with smart classrooms, dedicated departments for each engineering discipline, centralized air-conditioned library, seminar halls, auditorium with 1000+ seating capacity, and separate hostels for boys and girls. Sports facilities include cricket ground, basketball courts, volleyball courts, gymnasium, and indoor game rooms. The campus also has cafeteria, medical center, ATM, and 24/7 security.

Student Activities

Students actively participate in various clubs including coding club, robotics club, cultural club, sports club, and social service club. Annual technical fest attracts participants from colleges across the country. Regular workshops, hackathons, and placement training sessions ensure holistic development of students.
