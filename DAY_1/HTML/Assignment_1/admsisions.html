Scholarships Available

We offer merit-based scholarships up to 50% fee waiver for students with exceptional academic records. Government scholarships for SC/ST/OBC students are also facilitated. Financial assistance and education loans are arranged through partner banks. Students from economically weaker sections (EBC) can also apply for need-based fee concessions.

Reservation Policy

We follow the government-mandated reservation policy with seats reserved for SC (15%), ST (7.5%), OBC (27%), and Economically Weaker Sections (10%). Additionally, 5% seats are reserved for children of defense personnel and 3% for students with disabilities. Management quota seats are also available with direct admission based on merit.
