Career Prospects

Our graduates are placed in top companies like Google, Microsoft, Amazon, TCS, Infosys, Wipro, Accenture, and many more. The average salary package ranges from 6-12 LPA for undergraduate students and 8-15 LPA for postgraduate students. Many of our alumni have also gone on to pursue higher studies at prestigious universities abroad.

Learning Facilities

All courses benefit from our state-of-the-art infrastructure including advanced computer labs with latest software, well-equipped mechanical and electrical workshops, electronics and communication labs, dedicated AI/ML research center, digital library with access to international journals, and 24/7 internet connectivity across campus.
