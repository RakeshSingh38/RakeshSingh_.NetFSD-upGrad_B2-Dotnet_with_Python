Our Campus

Spread across 50 acres of lush green landscape, our campus provides a perfect blend of academic excellence and natural beauty. The campus houses state-of-the-art laboratories, a central library with over 50,000 books, modern auditoriums, sports complexes, and comfortable hostel facilities for both boys and girls.

Academic Excellence

Our curriculum is designed to meet industry standards and is regularly updated to incorporate the latest technological advancements. We emphasize practical learning through projects, internships, and industry visits. Our students consistently achieve excellent results with many securing top ranks in university examinations.

Career Opportunities

Our dedicated placement cell works year-round to connect students with leading companies. Top recruiters include TCS, Infosys, Wipro, Tech Mahindra, L&T, and many more. We maintain a consistent placement record of over 90% with competitive salary packages. Our alumni network spans across the globe, working in prestigious organizations.
